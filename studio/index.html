Small is beautiful

We are a design studio from Zagreb (Croatia). About a decade ago we left our jobs in big companies to dive deep into the creative process and create something that continues to influence
and inspire future generations.  Its about staying a student, exploring the depths of creativity, learning new skills, and loving your life.

Brand art

As a team we aim high, striving to create purpose-driven, conceptually strong, and visually distinctive brands that last for decades.

Our process is studious and thoughtful. It's a constant distillation of ideas with a goal to say the most with the least, and communicate something complex in a
simple way. It includes research, strategy, creative conceptualization, design, motion graphics, and audio production.
