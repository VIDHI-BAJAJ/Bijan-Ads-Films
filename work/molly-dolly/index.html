Services

Packaging, Photography

Client

Vino i gitare

Services

Packaging, Photography

Dolly & Molly are organic sparkling wines made with the traditional production method, known for the production of Champagne in France. That’s why we designed a label in the style of the “Old World” wines. We combined a rich typographic treatment with off-white paper, and the logo was applied as a stamp to complete the classic aesthetic. The third wine in the group is the unfiltered Pep-Nat, made with a similar natural process without added supplements. Since all the wines are organic, we reduced plastic materials in the packaging. Removing the foil wrap from the bottleneck made the bottle more transparent, which is an interesting contrast to the traditional ornamental style of the label.
